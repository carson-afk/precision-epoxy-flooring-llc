Residential Garage.

Most homeowners come to us with a 2-car or 3-car garage that's covered in oil stains, tire marks, and 30 years of wear. We grind it down to fresh concrete, fix the cracks, broadcast a custom flake blend, and seal it with polyaspartic. The result is a floor you'll actually want to show off.

What is included

Diamond grind prep. Crack and pit repair. Vinyl flake broadcast in your color blend. UV-stable polyaspartic top coat. Walk on it the next day. Drive on it in 24-48 hours.

Typical timeline

Most 2-car garages: 1 to 2 days. 3-car or shop garages: 2 to 3 days.

Typical price

2-car garages typically run $2,800-$4,800. 3-car runs $4,200-$6,800. Use our instant estimator for a tighter range.

Why flake

Hides every dirt mark. Adds slight texture for grip. Looks better than solid color in 90% of garages. Same durability as our other systems.
