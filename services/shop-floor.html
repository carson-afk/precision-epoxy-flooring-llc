Shop & Work Area.

Shops and work areas get the worst of it - oil drips, jack stands, dropped tools, equipment moves. We use the same flake system but with a thicker base coat and heavier broadcast for buildings that take real abuse.

Best for

Detached shops, workshops, hobby spaces, equipment storage, work areas of all kinds.

Heavier system

Thicker high-build base coat. Full broadcast (no thin spots). Sealed with chemical-resistant polyaspartic.

Color options

Standard blends from our deck or custom-match to your building. Industrial gray, charcoal, and warm earth tones are most common.

Typical price

$5-$9 per square foot installed depending on prep needed.
