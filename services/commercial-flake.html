Commercial Flake.

Retail spaces, showrooms, and small commercial buildings get a flake floor system designed for foot traffic plus easy cleaning. We can match your brand colors and add anti-slip aggregate to the top coat.

Best for

Auto showrooms, retail stores, restaurants (BOH), salons, gyms, light commercial.

Branded color match

We can blend flake to match your brand palette. Bring us a logo or sample and we'll mix to it.

Anti-slip option

Add aggregate to the top coat for slip resistance in food-service or wet areas.

Typical price

$7-$11 per square foot depending on prep, brand match, and add-ons.
