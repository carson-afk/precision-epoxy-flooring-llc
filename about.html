Eastern Idaho's epoxy pros.

200+ floors coated across Eastern Idaho. Decorative flake is our most-requested service, but we install the full lineup of epoxy floor systems.

Who we are.

We are a small, owner-operated epoxy floor company based in Idaho Falls. We have installed over 200 floors across Idaho Falls, Ammon, Rigby, Rexburg, Pocatello, and the surrounding cities.

Our specialty is decorative flake floors - it is what we install most often, and it is what most homeowners and shop owners ask us for. But we are full-system epoxy pros. Our crew knows how every brand of resin behaves in Idaho's temperature swings, which color blends look great in a 2-car garage versus a shop, and how to grind a 30-year-old slab and bring it back to life.

How we are different.

Diamond grind, every floor. We do not acid-etch. Mechanical bond is the only thing that lasts.

Polyaspartic top coats. UV-stable, chemical-resistant, faster cure, no yellowing. The standard for premium garage and basement floors.

10-year warranty. Idaho winters, salt, hot tires, dropped tools - we warranty against all of it.

Owner operated. The person quoting your floor is the person doing the work. No layers, no subs.
